🗃 Revision Cards

⚡ Quiz Mode
Test your knowledge with a robust 100-question bank featuring:

* Dynamic Sessions: 30 randomly selected questions per attempt.
* Anti-Rote Learning: Options are randomized using the Fisher-Yates shuffle.
* Exam Pressure: A 20-minute countdown timer that turns red at the 2-minute mark.
* Smooth Navigation: Previous/Next controls to manage your pace. [8, 9, 10, 11, 12] 

📊 Performance Tracking
After every quiz, you receive a full breakdown:

* Detailed Review: Compare your answers against the correct ones with included explanations.
* Smart Feedback:
* 90–100%: Excellent 🌟
   * 70–89%: Good 👍
   * 50–69%: Needs Improvement 📈
   * <50%: Revise the Notes 📖 [13]
